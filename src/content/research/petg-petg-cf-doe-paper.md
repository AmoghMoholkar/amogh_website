abstract: "This study examines whether short carbon-fiber reinforcement changes the deformation and failure mechanism of FDM-printed PETG, rather than simply scaling its mechanical properties. Using a full factorial design across layer thickness and infill density for both PETG and PETG-CF, specimens tested per ASTM D638 were monitored with digital image correlation (DIC) for full-field strain and examined post-fracture via SEM. A physically-constrained curve-fitting pipeline (R²-based elastic region detection, isotonic regression, cubic smoothing splines, and Brent's method for 0.2% offset yield) was used to extract consistent mechanical properties across all conditions."
